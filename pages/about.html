Meet the girl behind the bar

Hi friends, I’m Bree.

The founder and face behind Sunset Sips, and I’m so happy you’re here!

What started as a passion for bringing people together has grown into a trusted mobile bartending experience serving celebrations all across Arizona. In the last year alone, I’ve had the honor of serving 50+ incredible events and creating countless unforgettable memories.

My favorite part of what I do is creating an experience that feels personal to you — from custom drink menus and signature cocktails to the thoughtful details that make your celebration one of a kind.

Whether I’m serving a wedding, a corporate event, or an intimate backyard gathering, my goal is always the same: to create a seamless, memorable experience so you can relax, celebrate, and be fully present with your guests.

I’m so glad you’re here, and I can’t wait to raise a glass with you!

Cheers,
Bree
